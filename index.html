What are Stroller Passes and Care Vouchers?

Here at Disney, we want to make your time as enjoyable as possible. To help us do that, we have re-useable strollers that you can rent out while supplies last. We also have Child Care Vouchers so you can enjoy all the rides, worry free. When our customers are not using these vouchers, this is our program to give away free vouchers to families in need.
